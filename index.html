Built to connect. Built to stay connected.

Every other OpenCode app fails before you send your first message. Here's what we do differently.

🔌

Connects on the first try

Enter your server URL — HTTP or HTTPS, LAN IP or Tailscale address, with or without a password. We run a health check, auto-detect your protocol, and tell you exactly what's wrong if something's off. No cryptic error messages.

🔔

Done? We'll tell you.

Kick off a big refactor, lock your phone, go make coffee. The moment OpenCode finishes — or needs your input — you get a native notification. No polling, no keeping the screen on, no checking manually.

⚡

Stays connected when you switch apps

Mobile browsers kill your session the moment you check Slack. A native background service keeps your connection alive and picks back up instantly when you return. Your agent never drops mid-task.

🏗️

Native Kotlin. Not a web wrapper.

Built from scratch in Kotlin with Jetpack Compose. Not a React app jammed into a shell, not a Capacitor project, not a WebView. It looks and feels like an Android app because it is one.

Up and running in under a minute

No firewall rules to configure. No certificates to generate. No documentation rabbit holes.
